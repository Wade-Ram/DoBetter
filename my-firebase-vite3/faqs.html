FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Who owns DoBetter?

DoBetter was founded and developed by Wayden Ram — a young innovator passionate about helping students study smarter, access learning tools easily, and grow through digital education.

Is DoBetter free to use?

Yes! Basic study resources and learning guides are completely free for all users. However, premium resources such as advanced notes, interactive quizzes, and exclusive content may require a small upgrade or subscription.

What can I find on DoBetter?

DoBetter provides subject-specific learning material for students in multiple grades — including notes, summaries, study guides, and exam tips. You can access your profile page to track progress and save resources.

How do I create an account?

Go to the Login section on the home page, click on Sign Up, and fill in your details. You will then be able to log in and start exploring your subjects and study resources.

What should I do if I experience a problem?

If you face any issues or need support, visit the Contact page and send us a message, or email dobetter0111@gmail.com. We will assist you as soon as possible.

Can I use DoBetter on my phone?

Yes. DoBetter is designed to be mobile-friendly — you can access it easily on your smartphone, tablet, or computer anytime.
